about lynn

I believe in teaching the basics first and love teaching and watching as riders progress. I enjoy the challenge of breaking in and re-schooling horses with issues, working with the horse until it responds and understands what I want it to do. 

Whatever your Equestrian needs I am confident with my knowledge and experience I will be able to help you to achieve your goals.
