Farmstays

Come and experience life on a horse farm. Have lessons, help out around the stables, feeding and grooming the horsesand enjoy home cooked meals. A unique equine experience for all ages and experience levels.

Accomodation

Rotorua is world renowned for tourism and the Paradise Valley Horse Centre offers comfortable bunkhouse accommodation for riders and yards, stables and paddocks for horses. All conveniently located to access the best that Rotorua has to offer. All of the following attractions are within a 10 minute drive from Paradise Valley Horse Centre.
  • Agroventures – bungy, swoop, freefall extreme, jet boating
  • Zorb downhill adrenalin rush
  • Skyline Skyrides – gondola, luge, sky swing
  • Paradise Valley Springs Wildlife Park
  • Rainbow Springs and Kiwi Encounter
  • The Redwoods - Whakarewarewa Forest (Great for Hacking)
  • NZ Fish & Game Trout Hatchery
  • Wingspan Birds of Prey
  • Trout fishing streams
  • NZ Fish & Game Trout Hatchery
  • Lake Rotorua lakeside reserves and boat ramps
  • Ngongotaha Village
  • 10 minutes to city centre and other major Rotorua attractions
  • Tect Park All Terrain a bit further drive (Great for Hacking)
