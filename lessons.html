lessons

We have been teaching riding enthusiasts to ride for over 30 years. We pride ourselves on lessons being provided by experienced instructors.

what we teach

We offer lesson programs for all ages and levels. Whether you are a seasoned rider, completely new to the sport, or simply rekindling your love of riding, we have something to suit. Lessons can either be 1 on 1 or as part of a group at similar levels of ability.
  • The basics of horse care, grooming, paddocking, health, saddlery
  • The foundations of riding from ground to saddle, correct position and accurate aids for good control
  • Handling horses safely establishing a good foundation with simple exercises for horses and riders
  • Groundwork, roundpenning, lungeing, float loading etc
  • Establishing manners and a good working relationship with your horse
  • Principles of nutrition and feeding for meeting specific equine needs

holiday camps

We run five day camps every school holidays throughout the year. Being a small establishment, we pride ourselves on keeping our camps limited to small groups enabling us to focus on each riders needs. All campers spend time hands-on with the horses both riding and around the stables.

Our ridden time is instructional (not trekking) and where the focus is on control, position, and managing their mounts within the group setting.We offer campers the ability to ride in rain, hail and snow with our indoor arena allowing campers to live & breathe horses all day everyday.

Campers will also receive theory lessons from our qualified staff all in an effort to increase knowledge and prepare for pony club certificates.We cater for children with their own ponies and those without their own. When the day is done our campers can bunk down in our cozy bunkhouse and enjoy filling home cooked meals and more fun.

Loan your pony for school-holidays

Going away these holidays and want to know that your pony is in good hands? We have children that would love the opportunity to participate on our camps, learning under expert supervision how to care for and ride providing the added benefits of peace of mind for you and a fit pony on your return.
